Requirements

There is absolutely no previous experience necessary to come and practice with us. Our members come from all walks of life and differ just as much in experiences as they do in rank.

Club dues are $30 a semester for undergraduates and $40 a semester for university faculty. There is no dress code, although loose fitting athletic clothing is preffered. The white Karate uniform or "gi" is provided later.
