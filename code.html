Origins

Karate traces its modern history to Okinawa, an island chain south of mainland Japan. Karate, meaning "empty hand" was developed as a method of self-defence during a time when weapons were banned in Okinawa. Most elements of Karate were originally brought from China, although much is still unknown about China's influence and how far it reaches.
